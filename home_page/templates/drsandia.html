Meet Dr. Sandia

Dr. Watermelon Sandia was born and raised in Albuquerque, NM along with two siblings and three pigs. She attended Albuquerque Fake University and graduated in 1991 with a Bachelor of Science in Animal Science. She returned to AFU to complete her Master of Science in Anatomy and Physiology, followed by her Doctorate in Veterinary Medicine, received in 2000.

During veterinary school, she completed a course on grief counseling and became certified in veterinary acupuncture. After veterinary school, she decided to stay in Albuquerque and completed a one-year internship at Fake Animal Hospital. Following her internship, in 2001, she started her practice which is now known as Sandia Animal Hospital.

Dr. Sandia appreciates all current clients and any potential clients that reach out to inquire. She is so excited that you chose to bring your pet to SAH and allow us to provide the very best care. SAH staff believes in a 100% satisfaction experience for you and your pet. Dr. Sandia takes extra time to ensure compliance and productivity as well as continue to educate ourselves in all aspects of small animal care.
